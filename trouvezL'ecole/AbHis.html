ABOUT TROUVEZ L'ECOLE
TROUVEZ L'ECOLE

Trouvez l'ecole is a website that aims to bridge the gap between the student and their academic institution of choice.

For many families in East Africa and around the globe, the current state of education poses an important question of whether investing in a high cost institution for their child will result in them acquiring quality education.
One such criteria of evaluating the standards of the schools is by knowing which schools have passed the rigorous standards required by both IAPS (at preparatory level) and the Council of International Schools or CIS.
